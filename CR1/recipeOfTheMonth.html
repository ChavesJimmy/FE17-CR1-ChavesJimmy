The "Boeuf Bourguignon"
Lunch

Preparation time : 30 minutes
Cooking time : 2 hours

    INGREDIENTS

  • 1,6 kg Beef (in cubes 3cm)
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Garlic Gloves
  • 75cl Red Wine
  • 6 Carrots (cut in cubes)
  • Herbs (bouquet garni)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Flour

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat two tablespoons of butter in a large casserole. Fry the beef over a medium–high heat until nicely browned on all sides, turning every now and then and adding more butter if necessary. As soon as the beef is browned, put it on the side.
  2. Pour a little more butter into the casserole in which the beef was browned and fry the chopped onion over a low heat for 5–6 minutes, stirring often until softened. Stir the garlic into the pan and cook for 1 minute more.
  3. Put the beef back to the casserole and add five to six tablespoons of flour and mix vigorously until all the ingredients are coated.
  4. Cover it all with the wine, add the herbs, the carrots, salt and pepper. Stir it a bit to avoir the flour to stick at the bottom of the casserole.
  5. Cover the casserole and let it cook at small heat for about 2 hours.
  6. Check the consistent of the sauce after 1 hour 45 minutes and adjust the cooking time. The sauce should be thick and creamy.
*As side dishes we recommend potatoes or polenta
